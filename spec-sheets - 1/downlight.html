Network Expansion & Coverage Recommendations

For larger installation areas that require extended coverage, the team strongly recommends implementing dedicated wireless access points rather than relying on standard router ranges. During our field testing, the TP-Link EAP650 - Outdoor demonstrated exceptional performance, maintaining a lag-free connection over a 400-foot range.

Integrating this hardware into the project scope ensures a stable, highly reliable connection for all smart landscape fixtures while simultaneously upgrading the client's overall property Wi-Fi infrastructure.
