Safety Information
Safety Information
Read and follow all warnings before installation. Review with your compliance team prior to publication.
1
Install and wire the fixture in accordance with local electrical codes and applicable low-voltage landscape lighting requirements.
2
Use only with compatible 9–15 Vac low-voltage outdoor landscape lighting systems and properly rated transformers.
3
Do not exceed transformer load capacity. Use 32.30 VA per Morpheus fixture for load calculations unless Dauer publishes an updated value.
4
Do not submerge fixtures or connectors unless the specific accessory is rated for submersible use.
5
Do not modify the fixture, lens system, antenna, wiring, or RF / Bluetooth components.
6
Use outdoor-rated low-voltage cable and connectors appropriate for the installation environment.
7
Keep wireless devices within documented range during pairing and commissioning.
8
For service, firmware, or connectivity issues, contact Dauer or a qualified installer.

12
Network Expansion & Coverage Recommendations

For larger installation areas that require extended coverage, the team strongly recommends implementing dedicated wireless access points rather than relying on standard router ranges. During our field testing, the TP-Link Axios 3000 demonstrated exceptional performance, maintaining a lag-free connection over a 400-foot range.

Integrating this hardware into the project scope ensures a stable, highly reliable connection for all smart landscape fixtures while simultaneously upgrading the client's overall property Wi-Fi infrastructure.

Voice Control Setup

1. Every Morpheus fixture is individually addressable on your network. 
2. View its IP and MAC in Device Info to identify, ping, or troubleshoot the fixture from your router. 
3. Use a DHCP reservation if you need a stable address for integration with third-party controllers.
14
OTA Firmware Updates
When a firmware update is available, the Dauer Smart App displays a badge on the device tile. Tap Update and keep the fixture powered and within Wi-Fi range until the progress bar completes (typically 2–4 minutes). The fixture reboots automatically and restores its previous state.

16
Wi-Fi + Bluetooth Pairing
Reference · Ch X
Morpheus fixtures run Wi-Fi and Bluetooth simultaneously. Pair the fixture over Bluetooth within ~30 m for initial setup, then connect it to your 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network in the Dauer Smart App for remote control and cloud features. Both radios stay active, no toggling required.
